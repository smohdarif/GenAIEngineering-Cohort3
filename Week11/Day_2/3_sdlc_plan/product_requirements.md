# Product Requirements Document for News Aggregator App

## Product Vision and Mission Statement
**Vision:** To empower subject matter experts with a curated news experience that enhances their knowledge base and decision-making capabilities through personalized news aggregation.

**Mission:** To create a dynamic platform that seamlessly aggregates news articles tailored to the interests and expertise of SMEs, facilitating informed decision-making and knowledge sharing within their professional communities.

## Feature List with Detailed Descriptions

### 1. Personalized News Feed
- **Description:** Users can modify their news feed based on selected topics, ensuring that content is tailored to their specific interests.
- **User Story:** As an academic researcher, I want to filter news articles by research topics so that I can stay updated on my field without sifting through irrelevant content.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** The feed updates in real-time based on user selections.

### 2. User Authentication
- **Description:** Secure login and registration process for users to create and manage their personal profiles.
- **User Story:** As an industry expert, I want to securely access my account so that my personalized settings and bookmarks are saved.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Successful login and signup must require email verification.

### 3. Search Functionality
- **Description:** Users can search for articles using keywords, categories, or authors.
- **User Story:** As a freelance journalist, I want to search for specific topics so that I can find articles that inspire my writing.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Search results should update dynamically based on user input with autocomplete suggestions.

### 4. Bookmarking System
- **Description:** Allows users to save articles for later reading.
- **User Story:** As a user, I want to bookmark articles to read them later so that I donâ€™t lose important information.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Users can bookmark and unbookmark articles seamlessly.

### 5. Social Sharing Options
- **Description:** Integration with social media platforms for easy sharing of articles.
- **User Story:** As an industry expert, I want to share relevant articles with my peers on social media to engage in discussion.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Articles can be shared directly from the app to various social media platforms.

### 6. Alerts System
- **Description:** Users can set up custom alerts for new articles related to their selected interests.
- **User Story:** As an academic researcher, I want to receive notifications for new papers on my research topics so that I do not miss important updates.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Alerts are sent promptly via app notifications and/or email.

## Feature Prioritization using MoSCoW Method
- **Must Have:**
  - Personalized news feed
  - User authentication
  - Search functionality
- **Should Have:**
  - Bookmarking system
  - Alerts system
- **Could Have:**
  - Social sharing options
- **Won't Have:**
  - Podcasts or video integration (for future phases)

## Integration Requirements
- Integration with third-party news APIs to pull in relevant articles based on user interests and preferences.

## Data Requirements and User Permissions
- User data must include:
  - Basic profile information
  - Preferences for news topics and sources
  - Bookmark history
- Permissions for data usage should be clearly articulated during the signup process, ensuring compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
