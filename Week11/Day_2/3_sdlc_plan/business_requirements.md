# Business Requirements Document for News Aggregator App

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the business requirements for a news aggregator app aimed at serving subject matter experts (SMEs). The application will aggregate relevant news articles and provide a personalized user experience, enabling SMEs to stay informed and make well-informed decisions. The analysis includes stakeholder identification, user personas, functional and non-functional requirements, business rules, success metrics, risk strategies, and regulatory considerations.

## Stakeholder Analysis
We have identified the following key stakeholders for the news aggregator app:

1. **End Users (Subject Matter Experts)**: Individuals requiring tailored news aggregation based on their expertise.
2. **Investors**: Financial stakeholders interested in the commercial viability of the app.
3. **Content Providers**: News organizations and independent journalists contributing articles.
4. **Technical Team**: Developers responsible for the app's technical architecture and functionality.
5. **Marketing Team**: Professionals tasked with promoting the app and ensuring it meets market needs.

### Stakeholder Needs
- **End Users**:
  - Personalized news feed tailored to specific fields of interest.
  - Features such as bookmarking, sharing, and save-for-later options.
  - A user-friendly interface that allows intuitive navigation.

- **Investors**:
  - A robust revenue model and clear marketing strategy.
  - Clear market demand evidence and competitive advantages.

- **Content Providers**:
  - Submission guidelines and metrics for performance assessment.
  - Engagement insights around the articles they provide.

- **Technical Team**:
  - Comprehensive specifications for features and user journeys.
  - Scalability and integration opportunities with existing news APIs.

- **Marketing Team**:
  - Access to user personas for targeted campaigns.
  - User analytics insights for effective engagement strategies.

## User Personas
### Persona 1: The Academic Researcher
- **Demographics**: Age 30-50, highly educated, in academia or research.
- **Goals**: Keep abreast of the latest research and relevant news; share findings with peers and students.
- **Needs**: Advanced filtering options; ability to bookmark articles for discussion.

### Persona 2: The Industry Expert
- **Demographics**: Age 25-55, professional in industries like technology, finance, healthcare.
- **Goals**: Stay informed on industry trends; leverage information for informed decision-making.
- **Needs**: Custom alerts for specific topics; networking integrations; concise articles.

### Persona 3: The Freelance Journalist
- **Demographics**: Age 20-40, independent worker covering diverse topics.
- **Goals**: Seek inspiration for articles and keep ahead of breaking news.
- **Needs**: Access to a wide range of news sources; tools for monitoring trends.

## Functional Requirements
1. **Personalized News Feed**: 
   - Users can customize their feed based on selected topics.
   - Users can save articles to their profiles.

2. **User Authentication**: 
   - Secure login and sign-up processes to create personal profiles.

3. **Search Functionality**: 
   - Users can search for news articles based on keywords, categories, or authors.

4. **Bookmarking System**: 
   - Users can bookmark articles for later reading.

5. **Social Sharing Options**: 
   - Integration with social media platforms for article sharing.

6. **Alerts System**: 
   - Users can set up alerts for new articles related to their interests.

### Acceptance Criteria
- Each functional requirement must pass user acceptance testing (UAT).
- User feedback will guide the prioritization of additional features.

## Non-Functional Requirements
1. **Performance**: 
   - The app must load within 3 seconds under normal load conditions.

2. **Security**: 
   - User data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.

3. **Scalability**: 
   - The app should handle up to 10,000 concurrent users.

## Business Rules
1. User-generated content must adhere to community guidelines.
2. All articles must be credited to their original sources to maintain credibility.
3. Advertising must align with user interests based on their personas.

## Constraints
1. Budget limitations may affect feature enhancements and marketing strategies.
2. Regulatory compliance with data protection laws and copyright regulations.

## Success Metrics and KPIs
1. **User Adoption Rate**: Number of active users in the first three months post-launch.
2. **Engagement Metrics**: Average time spent on the app and number of bookmarked articles.
3. **Retention Rate**: Percentage of users returning within a month after initial usage.
4. **Revenue Generation**: Income from advertisements and any potential subscription models.

## Risk Analysis and Mitigation Strategies
1. **Technical Failure**: Regular maintenance and updates; robust testing phases.
2. **Market Competition**: Continuous market analysis; adapt features based on user feedback.
3. **Regulatory Non-compliance**: Ongoing review of compliance requirements; legal consultations.

## Regulatory and Compliance Considerations
- User data collection must comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
- Content sourcing must respect copyright laws and attribution requirements.

This comprehensive business requirements document will serve as a roadmap for the development team, ensuring alignment between business objectives, user needs, and technical feasibility.
